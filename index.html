About Us

What Makes Our Coffee Special?

Coffee can be special because of the coffee beans, the way they are grown, and the roasting process. Specialty coffee beans are carefully sourced and roasted, and are often grown at higher altitudes.

Farmers take extra care to harvest the beans at the right time to achieve the best flavor.Professional quality graders evaluate green coffee beans for defects and sort them. Roasted beans are evaluated for taste, acidity, sweetness, and body.

The way coffee is stored can also affect its flavor.The country where the coffee is grown can also affect its flavor.The variety of coffee bean can also affect its flavor.
